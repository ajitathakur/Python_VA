VIRTUAL ASSISTANT

It understands your voice commands, searches news and knowledge sources, and summarizes and reads out content to you.

This application is under deployment.

Press the Query button to issue a voice query. You might be prompted to allow microphone access.

You can issue voice queries of the following forms as of now.

Mathematics

  • Basic Mathematics
  • Compute Algebraic Equations
  • Trigonometry
  • Mathematical Conversions

Note: The current version supports NY Times and The Guardian as sources. Unknown sources will default to NY Times. Also, the source is expected to be mentioned at the end of the query. You could say NY Times or nyt instead of the full name.

Knowledge

  • Albert Einstein
  • Thomas Edison
  • Prime Minister's Of Countries
  • World Wars

Science

  • Alloys
  • Physics
  • Physical Measurements
  • Weights and Masses

English

  • Word Meanings
  • Synonyms Of words
  • Antonyms Of words
  • And a lot more !!

And if you're getting bored !!

  • Here's a virtual friend that will listen to your talks and guess what !! It never gets bored.

Note: The current version uses Wikipedia API for knowledge queries. There might be an occasional lengthy lag.
